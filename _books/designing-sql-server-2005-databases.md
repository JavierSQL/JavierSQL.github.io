Microsoft Official Curriculum (Course 2782A) — a two-day, instructor-led course on designing SQL Server 2005 databases from business requirements: gathering requirements and forming a conceptual design, evaluating the logical design, physical-design best practices, designing for scalability, and data-access strategy. Javier Loria co-authored the course, writing its content; the instructional design was led by another author.
