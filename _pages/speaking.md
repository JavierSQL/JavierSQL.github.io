I speak on semantic models, data modeling, and architecting analytics on the
Microsoft data platform — practical sessions aimed at practitioners, not
product tours. I present in English or Spanish, in person or remote.

## Topics I present

- **Semantic models on Microsoft Fabric and Power BI** — modeling for analytics
  that scale, and the design decisions behind them.
- **Data warehousing and T-SQL in practice** — the patterns and trade-offs that
  hold up in production.
- **AI in the data workflow** — MCP, Claude Code, and AI-assisted requirements
  for data teams.

## Background

I spoke internationally on the Microsoft data stack with SolidQ, and I've spent
the last 25 years designing data solutions across Latin America while building
[Primus Data](https://primusdata.net) in Costa Rica. I'm a well-known voice in
the Spanish-speaking data community and I'm now bringing that work to
English-speaking audiences.
